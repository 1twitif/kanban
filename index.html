Instant project management for your Gitlab issues

Leanlabs kanban is built from the ground up using the Gitlab public API. Kanban issues are Gitlab issues, you will never have to deal with syncronization problems. Keep issues where they belong, in the repository with your code!

Fully customizable task board gives developers instant feedback into the status of tasks thoughout your progress.

Easy deployment in less then 5 minutes, install on your own servers or any cloud provider supporting Docker

You control who can see your repositories. We respect the same permissions you have setup with your Gitlab repositories
